About

This website helps twitter users quench their thirst for Favorites and Retweets by optimizing how and when they post. Quenchr compare the sentiment, content, length, and contained media (e.g links, images) of a proposed tweet to other popular tweets containing the same keywords to find what results in the most activity. Our website also gives our users the option to have our site post a given Tweet to their Twitter at the optimal time for them. {%endblock%}
